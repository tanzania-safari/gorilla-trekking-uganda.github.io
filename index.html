Where Does Gorilla Trekking in Uganda Take Place?

Gorilla Trekking Uganda primarily takes place in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. The park is divided into four trekking sectors: Rushaga, Nkuringo, Buhoma, and Ruhija.

Each sector offers unique terrain, scenery, and gorilla families. Rushaga has the highest number of habituated families, while Nkuringo offers dramatic mountain views.

The experience is intimate, regulated, and conservation-focused. Gorilla trekking permits in Uganda cost approximately $800 per person, making Uganda more affordable than gorilla tracking Rwanda.
